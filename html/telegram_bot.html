crypto telegram bot

over the crypto winter of Spring 2022, I found myself reading Darren Lau's Daily Ape mailing list on telegram. i would read about a bunch of promising crypto-assets, but had no formalized way of recording them to monitor their performance. i decided to combine the process of logging and tracking the crypto assets by creating a CRUD app that integrated with the telegram bot api and pymongo, a MongoDB api for python. here is an example of the frontend.
