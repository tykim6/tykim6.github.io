sports analytics project

i collaborated with some friends on a group project for a class, 15.285 (Sports Strategy and Analytics). We decided to model a baseball hitter's decision of which pitch to "sit on" (guessing what the pitcher is going to throw) as an algorithm that takes as input a hitter's success against different pitches and a pitcher's liklihood of throwing those pitches, to output a decision of which pitch to "sit on".
